NouNou Farin Lakte cream

Description

NOUNOU Farin Lakte cream with wheat flour and milk, is a well-balanced complementary food for babies after the 6th month. It contains essential vitamins and minerals in ideal proportions that help the proper development of infants and young children. Contains gluten.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsps of nounou farin lakte powder
  • a bowl of warm milk

Steps

  1. Warm a bowl of milk
  2. Put 1 tablespoon of powder and mix. Repeat 5 times
